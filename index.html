Rent a Travel Trailer on Vancouver Island

A Vancouver Island outdoor holiday experience awaits you! Make the outdoors your base of discovery in the comfort of a new travel trailer.

There are excellent campsites in the Southern Island area. You can relax and sleep among 100' and 100 year old trees, yet all amenities are close by.

Find Out More

It couldn't be easier. When you book with us the trailer is delivered and set up at your desired campsite. When you are finished we pick it up and take it away. Easy. We just ask you leave the trailer as you found it.

We make sure that everything is fully inspected, ready and safe for your use. In fact, as we are transporting the trailer, delivery and take away is not something you need to worry about.

We want you have the best holiday experience. Need that something extra? Just ask!

Comfort, simplicity, ease. discovery and relaxation are words that come to mind when thinking of vacation objectives. We offer both services and extras to make your experience extraordinary. Just looking for local area activities? Just ask.
